Mileage climbing. Diagnostic codes firing. Oil life depleting. Battery levels dropping. Lease milestones approaching. That data exists — your dealership just can't see it.

With CarConnective

A live connection to every vehicle you've ever sold.

CarConnective connects directly to the vehicle's onboard computer systems through secure automotive APIs. No hardware installs. No dongles. No GPS trackers. No OBD devices. How it works

From setup to automation
in five steps.

CarConnective is designed for speed. Your dealership can be live and connected within days — not months.

01

Dealership setup

We configure your CarConnective instance — branding, modules, team access, notification preferences, and DMS integrations. Your platform is ready before your first vehicle connects.

02

Customer invitation

After every sale, your team sends a branded connection invite — via your dealership app or a simple SMS link. The customer taps once, authenticates with their vehicle manufacturer, and authorises data sharing. Under two minutes. Zero friction.

03

Vehicle connection

The vehicle's onboard computer systems connect to CarConnective through secure automotive APIs. Odometer, fuel, battery, diagnostics, service status, and location (where supported) begin flowing automatically. No hardware. No installs. No maintenance.

04

Intelligence activation

CarConnective's AI engine begins analysing every data point. At-risk vehicle scoring, trade-in likelihood, campaign timing optimisation, and alert prioritisation activate immediately. Your dashboard becomes a live intelligence feed.

05

Automation takes over

Service alerts trigger appointment bookings. Mileage milestones trigger trade-in offers. Lease expiry dates trigger upgrade conversations. Diagnostic codes trigger customer outreach. Every signal becomes an automated, revenue-generating action.
